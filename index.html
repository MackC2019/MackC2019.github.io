Mind-wave Cursor Control

More detail at my Github page here

The block diagram above explains the structure of project. This project involves advanced techniques of Digital signal process, Machine Learning, and common algorithm in Neuroscience.

The objective of this project is to control cursor movement on the computer by human intention through brain computer interface (BCI). This will include an electroencephalography (EEG) data collection from EEG monitor. The monitor is expected to track the electrical activity of the brain (mainly focus on motor cortex, the part of brain oversees movement). The project is mainly focusing on classify left or right movement for now. The completed project allows subjects to think about executing cursor movement with corresponding hand movement and covert signal to real-time command.

Jinxiu City

.

Planned net land area: 45810.31 ㎡. Total construction area: 291035.41 ㎡.The project consists 12 high-rise residential buildings and 1 high-rise office building. The most difficulty of the project lies in the large height difference between the east and west sides of the site, which brings great difficulty to the design of underground garage, because multiple ramps need to be set to solve the height difference problem, but the ramps will take up too many parking Spaces, resulting in the insufficient number of parking Spaces. Through detailed calculation of earthwork, I worked out an optimized excavation and filling plan to reduce the height difference between the two sides of the site. This reduced the number and length of ramps. Then I repeatedly adjusted the design of the underground garage, by adjusting the boundary range, the size of each building shear wall and building location, optimize the design of equipment rooms and other means, to arrange parking spaces as much as possible, successfully completed the design.
